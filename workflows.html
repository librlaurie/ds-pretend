Establish sustainable workflows for digital publishing and digital project management.

Together with colleagues in the library, the Price Lab, and across campus, develop community of practice for supporting digital projects (technologies, procedures, etc)

  • Create project checklists and workflows for cross-use with schools/center/ Price Lab
  • Create a data triage team. This team would take requests to help researchers extract their data from old systems and advise on where it could move to, considering what would be best for long term preservation.

  • Create and use an intake process/checklist for digital scholarship projects/activities
  • Develop and support tiered service models to answer question: where should projects be stored/hosted/served while they are in development/production?.
  • Create several use cases for production level server environments to recommend and employ for digital projects. For example Use docker plus digital ocean as a development environment for digital projects.
